how do i do the cool code stuff you have

you can learn to code like me if you know how to read and write. that includes understanding "if-then" sentences and the word "and", then you understand the complete foundations of programming.

the worst part about this though is that many programmers give off this vibe of "watch out! only real geniuses only", but you can legitimately learn on your own and get help from people if you know ask for it properly and you don't ask your question in the first message you send. the good news is, when you learn everything about programming, you don't have to pay for all these extra website building services or whatnot. why pay when you can make it yourself?

super fabulous developer Nicky Case answered this better on their FAQ page, so please read that because they explain it heck of a lot better than i can
